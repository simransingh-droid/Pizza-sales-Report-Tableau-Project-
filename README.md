# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analytics Dashboard

### *Tableau | Business Intelligence | Data Visualization*

---

## 📌 Project Summary

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of pizza sales data using **Tableau**, aimed at uncovering actionable business insights to enhance sales performance and operational efficiency.

Acting as a Business Intelligence Analyst, the objective was to transform raw transactional data into an interactive dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify trends, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze sales patterns across time (daily, weekly, hourly)
* Identify peak business hours and customer demand cycles
* Evaluate top-performing and underperforming products
* Measure key metrics such as revenue, order volume, and average order value
* Provide strategic recommendations to improve profitability and efficiency

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Tableau Desktop** – Data visualization & dashboard development
* **Microsoft Excel / SQL** – Data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing
* **Business Analytics Techniques** – Trend analysis, KPI tracking, and performance evaluation

---

## 📂 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains detailed transactional records of pizza sales, including:

* **Order Details**: Order ID, Date, Time
* **Product Information**: Pizza Name, Category, Size, Ingredients
* **Sales Metrics**: Quantity, Unit Price, Total Price

This structured dataset enables multi-dimensional analysis across time, product categories, and revenue metrics.

## 📈 Key Insights

* Peak sales are observed during **weekends and evening hours**, indicating strong demand during leisure periods
* Order volume significantly increases during **lunch and dinner time slots**
* A small group of products contributes disproportionately to total revenue (high-performing SKUs)
* Certain items consistently underperform, highlighting opportunities for menu optimization

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

* **Optimize Staffing**: Allocate more staff during peak hours to improve service efficiency
* **Menu Engineering**: Promote high-margin and high-demand products strategically
* **Product Optimization**: Reevaluate or reposition underperforming items
* **Targeted Marketing**: Leverage peak-time promotions and combo offers
* **Operational Efficiency**: Streamline processes during high-demand periods
